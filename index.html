Alpha Technologies Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Alpha Technologies ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you access or use our websites, applications, products, services, and other digital properties (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of our Services immediately.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Policy applies to all data collected by Alpha Technologies through its Services, including information provided directly by you and data obtained from third-party sources or partner organizations. This Policy applies globally but may be supplemented by jurisdiction-specific disclosures as required by applicable law.

3. Information We Collect

3.1. Information You Provide Directly

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as when you create an account, request support, or engage with our Services. This may include:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and account credentials
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Professional or organizational information, if applicable
  • Any correspondence or feedback you provide to us

3.2. Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Access times, pages viewed, and referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies used to enhance functionality

3.3. Information from Partners

We may receive information from trusted partner organizations with whom we collaborate. These partners may include analytics services, advertising networks, or affiliated technology providers. Any such data exchange occurs under strict contractual terms ensuring appropriate data protection.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to operate, improve, and secure our Services. Specifically, your information may be used to:

  • Provide access to our Services and maintain account functionality
  • Personalize your experience and content recommendations
  • Develop, test, and enhance our products and technologies
  • Communicate with you regarding updates, offers, and service-related notices
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies

Alpha Technologies may occasionally share certain user data with partner companies for operational, analytical, or commercial purposes. Such partners are pre-approved and bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements. We do not sell or trade personal information to random or unrelated entities.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Our processing of personal information is based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent, where required
  • Performance of a contract to which you are a party
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests, such as service improvement and fraud prevention

6. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your information under the following circumstances:

  • To affiliated companies or subsidiaries for legitimate business purposes
  • To third-party service providers performing functions on our behalf
  • To partner organizations who have entered into data cooperation agreements with us
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal processes, or governmental requests

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by applicable law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted, anonymized, or archived in compliance with data retention standards.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, network firewalls, and secure data storage systems, to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure, and users should exercise caution when sharing information online.

9. Data Sharing and Partner Relationships

Alpha Technologies maintains partnerships with certain trusted companies that assist in analytics, infrastructure, or advertising. Occasionally, aggregated or anonymized data may be shared with these partners to fulfill business objectives. Any such transfers are conducted in accordance with data protection regulations and under strict contractual safeguards.

We may, upon legitimate request, provide specific partners with user information for research, technical collaboration, or service improvement. Such data exchanges are governed by agreements ensuring the responsible use and protection of information. We do not sell or license user data to unknown or unaffiliated parties.

10. International Data Transfers

Where applicable, Alpha Technologies may transfer personal data to countries outside your jurisdiction for operational purposes. In such cases, we ensure that appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms) are implemented to maintain data protection standards.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access, review, or request copies of your data
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate information
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent, where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@alphatech.com. We will verify your identity before fulfilling any request to protect your privacy and security.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, beacons, and similar technologies to improve user experience and analyze website performance. You may control or delete cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect functionality.

13. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

14. Updates to This Policy

Alpha Technologies reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any material changes will be communicated through our Services or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent revision occurred.
